Carl the Penguin lived in the house of Cheese. Even though he was lactose and tolerant. His friends always asked him why he lived there. Carl was stubborn and never listened to his friends.

For his love hate relationship of cheese was what made him who he was. He knew that he would never be tempted to eat his house because of how much he hated the cheese. It was the perfect house material for him.

But one day, Carl the Penguin had enough.
